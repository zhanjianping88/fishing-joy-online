About Ocean Arcade Game

Ocean Arcade Game is a small hobby site built around one fish game I kept wanting to tidy up, plus a few side pages and route ideas that made the whole thing feel more alive.

What this site is for

The goal is simple: open the page, see the game fast, and maybe come back later because one route or one post stuck in your head. I am not trying to turn it into a giant directory. I would rather keep it small and actually maintain it.

Open source foundation

The featured fishing game is adapted from the open source repository imtonyjaa/ggemu-fishing-joy. The original MIT license and attribution are preserved on the license page, and I keep that credit visible because it matters.
